D ox refers to the diffusion coefficient of the oxidant (hexachloroiridate(IV) or hexacyanoferrate(III), D red refers to that of the reductant (hexachloroiridate(III) or hexacyanoferrate(II)). The actual concentration of hexachloroiridate(III) was not measured directly using UV-VIS, it was estimated based on the amount dissolved and the loss of concentration of hexachloroiridate(IV) (also see 2.0 experimental).
