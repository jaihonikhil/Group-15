Level of evidence A: data derived from multiple randomized clinical trials or meta-analyses, B: data derived from a single randomized clinical trial or large non-randomized studies, C: Consensus of opinion of the experts and/or small studies, retrospective studies, registries. Classification magnitude of effect and levels of evidence is partly adapted from ESC/EAS guidelines ( ). Magnitude of effect: ++ = between 5% and 10%, + = less than 5%, - = no effect.
