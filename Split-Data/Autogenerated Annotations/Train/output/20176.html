FP rhinoconjunctivitis was defined as having sneezing, running nose, stuffy nose, or eyes tingling and tears when the child contacts with animals or pets during past 12 months. FP diagnosed rhinitis was defined as the child having doctor-diagnosed rhinitis with FP symptoms. Other diagnosed rhinitis was defined as the child having doctor-diagnosed rhinitis without FP symptoms. P-values were calculated by Chi-2 statistics between FP rhinoconjunctivitis and FP wheeze.
