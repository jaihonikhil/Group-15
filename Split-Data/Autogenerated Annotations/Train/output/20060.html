Culture medium. The culture medium for fibroblasts consisted of Dulbecco's modified Eagle medium (DMEM, Sigma–Aldrich, St Louis, MO, USA) supplemented with 50 U/ml penicillin (Penicillin, Gibco BRL, Life Technologies, Paisley, UK), 50 mg/ml streptomycin (Streptomycin Gibco BRL, Life Technologies, Paisley, UK) and 10% fetal calf serum (NCS, Gibco BRL, Life Technologies, Paisley, UK). For keratinocytes, culture medium consisted of DMEM supplemented with 50 U/ml penicillin, 50 mg/ml, streptomycin, 1 mg/l epidermal gtrth factor (EGF, Sigma–Aldrich, St Louis, MO, USA) and 25 mg/l bovine pituitary extract (BPE, Sigma–Aldrich, St Louis, MO, USA) was used.
