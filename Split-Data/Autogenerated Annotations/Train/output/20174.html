SAPALDIA: Swiss Cohort Study on Air Pollution and Lung and Heart Diseases in Adults. Lden: day-evening-night noise level. Depression defined as doctor-diagnosed depression or use of medication or SF-36 mental health score <50. N = 4,581 for all variables except for b (n = 4,558), c (n = 4,191), d (n = 3,982) and e (n = 4,555). Doctor-diagnosed chronic disease defined as self-reported physician diagnosis of asthma, chronic bronchitis, hypertension, cardiovascular disease, diabetes, stroke, migraine, and arthritis. Overweight/obesity defined as body mass index > 25 kg/m 2 . Daytime sleepiness defined as scoring > 10 on the Epworth’s sleepiness scale.
