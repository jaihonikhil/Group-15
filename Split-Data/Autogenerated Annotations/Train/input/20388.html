Suitability of the different strategies for certain wind- and site conditions and general tendencies of the strategies for the estimation of ventilation rates and ammonia emissions. C O 2 i n and N H 3 i n are carbon dioxide and ammonia concentrations for the inside concentration values, C O 2 o u t and N H 3 o u t for the outside concentration values. Scenario Sc1 : straight and stable flow through the barn, either cross or lateral. Sc2 : unstable and weak wind conditions and ambiguous wind directions. Sc3 : building and wind combination leading to flow deflection or complex flow pattern (e.g. long barns with mainly lateral flow, large flow obstacles inside the barn). Sc4 : incoming flow is contaminated with gas concentrations from different outside sources. Sc5 : measurement campaigns with multiple barns with different geometries and wind conditions.
