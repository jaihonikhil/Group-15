Cisplatin has a dual-effect on cancerogenesis, which hinges on the type of cancer and the circadian genes involved.
Cancer Type Circadian Gene Cancer Reference
Leukemia BMAL1 Increase
Breast Cancer CLOCK, CRY1, CRY2, PER1, PER2, PER3 Decrease
Lung Cancer CLOCK, PER1, PER2, PER3 Decrease
Ovarian Cancer CLOCK Decrease
