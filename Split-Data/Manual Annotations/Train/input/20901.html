Blood samples of 32 menstrual cycles from a group of 20 women with and without premenstrual syndrome [ . J Clin Endocrinol Metab, 81 , 1076-82]. For the reference cycle, women provided daily blood samples for progesterone and allopregnanolone assays on cycle days 1–4, and from cycle day 10 throughout the remaining cycle, and furhter until the 4 th day of menstrual bleeding. Between cycle days 4 and 10, occasional blood samples were taken. The average age of the women was 36.6 years (range 25–44 years). All cycles in the reference group were ovulatory, as defined by plasma progesterone values exceeding 15 nmol/l.
