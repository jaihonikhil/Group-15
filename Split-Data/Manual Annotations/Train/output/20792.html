Areal surface texture parameters measured in this study.
Parameter Description
Sa Arithmetic mean height of selected area
Sq Root-Mean-Square height of selected area
Sv Maximum valley depth of selected area
Ssk Skewness of selected area. It describes how the mass is distributed around the mean plane.
Sku Kurtosis of selected area. It is a measure of the sharpness of the surface roughness over the area.
